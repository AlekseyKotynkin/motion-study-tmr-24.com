Component repair and restoration of electronic printed circuit boards.


Component repair and restoration of electronic printed circuit boards allows you to significantly save on replacing them in the kit, which gives additional benefits compared to the purchase of new boards.

It can significantly reduce the cost of replacing the board and help save critical parts and equipment.

Component repairs and refurbishments of printed circuit boards can also prevent the replacement of expensive parts and equipment, such as servers and control systems.



We remind you that contacting an unqualified specialist may lead to a complete failure of the electronic board or an increase in the cost of its restoration.


  • Diagnosis of the problem.

  • Removing the problematic component from the board.

  • Checking neighboring components for damage.

  • Replacement of the problematic component.

  • Checking the board on the diagnostic machine.

  • Checking the performance of the board.

  • Checking the health of the device.

Component repair of printed circuit boards ensures the accuracy and stability of troubleshooting and prevents the consequences of malfunctions in the future.


In some cases, we are ready to offer customers a repair option based on used spare parts. This may be caused by the need to minimize the cost of repairs, or in the absence of the necessary element base (for example, industrial equipment has long been discontinued and is not supported by the manufacturer).

Repair of automated lines and robots.


Regardless of the type of equipment, almost any component can break, up to the control controller or resistor. Some parts fail due to contamination or exposure to moisture. In any case, if the automated line stops, the company incurs losses, so repairs need to be made in the shortest possible time.

If you are interested in professional repair of automated lines and robots, please contact us. We guarantee you quality service and support at any time.

Our team of professional specialists is ready to do everything necessary to restore timely and high-quality production work of the automated line.



The main reasons for the failure of automated lines:


  • Natural wear of components and parts.

  • Improper maintenance and repair of equipment.

  • Non-standard and emergency situations.

  • Errors in production data or information.

  • Mechanical damage to the line.

We work at various production facilities, restore structurally complex equipment in the shortest possible time, regardless of its condition.


We carry out both block repairs with a complete replacement of the node, and component repairs with the replacement of a separate part.
